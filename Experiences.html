Experiences

SHAD MtA 2018

In 2018, I attended SHAD at Mount Allison University, NB. Over 27 days, I experienced a new level of independence with 48 other curious and like minded individuals.

With a busy schedule, I was exposed to groundbreaking topics through powerful lectures, creative labs and lessons. I also loved the variety of extracurricular activities such as unicycling, fencing, programming and surgical suturing.

SHAD taught me to seize the day, question norms and take risks. Approaching life with a 'why not' attitude has widened my opportunities and prepared me to take on further challenge!
